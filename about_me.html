Lightning McQueen

Montgomery "Lightning" McQueen is the main character and central

protagonist of the long-running and highly successful Cars brand.

He was created in the early 2000s by Pixar's design artists,

and has become a popular mascot for his home franchise

as well as Disney and Pixar in general. Having had

a debut in the 2006 animated feature film Cars he

McQueen is a professional racecar who quickly had

rose to prominence in his local scene, before he

again debuting in the Piston Cup as the #95 Rust

-eze driver.
