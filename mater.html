Mater

Sir Tow Mater, better known as Mater,is the

deuteragonist of the Cars franchise. In the

franchise.In the films, he is a

major character in Cars,the main

protagonist of Cars 2 and a

supporting character in Cars 3.

In the TV shows, he is the titular

protagonist of his own Tall tales

series, one of the main characters

of Tales From Radiator Springs and one of the two

main protagonists of Cars on the Road.
